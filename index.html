How to use My Timeline

A personal countdown page that lives in your browser — no account, no internet needed. Add milestones, watch the time count down. Your data is stored locally, so clearing your browser data or switching browsers will remove your milestones.

+
Adding milestones
Tap Add Milestone, fill in a name and date, optionally a time and category colour, then hit Save.
⊙
Pinning
Hover a card to reveal action buttons. ⊙ Pin keeps a milestone at the top regardless of date.
✎
Editing & deleting
Hover a card for ✎ edit and ✕ delete. Deleted milestones can be recovered via Undo for a few seconds.
●●
Categories & filtering
Each colour is a category. With two or more, filter chips appear above the timeline to show or hide categories. Name them in ⚙ Options → Category names.
⚙
Options
Change the count unit, toggle decimals, hide past milestones, sort, and switch themes.
◐
Dark mode
Toggles dark mode. Saved automatically.
📅
Exporting
Download all milestones as a .ics file from ⚙ Options → Export, importable into Apple Calendar, Google Calendar, and others. One-time snapshot, not a live sync.
Sharing
The share button has two sections. Your milestones — copies a link that lets anyone import your current milestones into their timeline. The app — sends a blank My Timeline to a friend so they can set up their own.
